I love biking, especially in Astoria Park!! Cycling/biking is a great way to get to where you want to go, while lowering one's carbon footprint. Biking is a much more sustainable type of transportation than other methods because of the lack of harmful emissions in the atmosphere and the positive contribution to climate change. Cycling keeps our hearts pumping and is a good form of cardio exercise. Biking is also typically known as a way to reduce stress, which helps us with our mental well being. While some people feel anxiety when cycling in the streets due to some traffic and not all streets having bike lanes, overall, biking is a way to release stress and tension for most people who envelop this lifestyle. Astoria Park, located in Astoria, Queens, NYC, has designated bike lanes making it ideal for people who want to ride worry free!
