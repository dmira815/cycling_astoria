I love biking, especially in Astoria Park!! Cycling/biking is a great way to get to where you want to go, while lowering one's carbon footprint. Biking is a much more sustainable type of transportation than other methods because of the lack of harmful emissions in the atmosphere that in turn will not contribute to climate change. Cycling keeps our hearts pumping and is a good form of cardio exercise. Biking is also typically known as a way to reduce stress, which helps us with our mental well being, although, some people feel anxiety when cycling in the street since most times there is some traffic and not all streets have bike lanes. Astoria Park, located in Astoria, Queens, NYC, has designated bike lanes making it ideal for people who want to ride worry free!
